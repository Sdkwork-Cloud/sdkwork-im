# S07 Loop77 Addendum - 2026-04-11

## 1. Main Conclusion
- Loop77 fixed a more serious ownership bug than the deferred scheduler work: next healthy ready-pair writes could bypass active pending claim ownership and silently flush operator-claimed backlog.
- The auto-retry queue now respects active and legacy-untracked claim ownership.
- Unclaimed backlog still retries automatically on the next healthy ready-pair write.
